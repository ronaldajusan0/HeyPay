Features

Crypto-simple. Merchant-easy. No new rails required.

Four pieces doing one job: turn your XLM into a payment a Philippine merchant already accepts.

qr_code_scanner

Scan any QRPH code

Point your camera at the QR any QRPH merchant already displays. We decode it server-side and find the merchant.

account_balance_wallet

Pay from your XLM

Prefund a custodial Stellar wallet once. Spend from that balance at any merchant — no off-ramp detour.

trending_up

Live rate, locked in

See the exact XLM→PHP quote before you confirm. The rate locks with a TTL, so what you approve is what you pay.

verified

Settled to their bank

We sell the XLM and pay out pesos to the merchant's registered bank account. They receive PHP — nothing new on their side.

How it works

Three taps from scan to settled.

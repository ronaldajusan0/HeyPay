## Image Prompt #1 — Slide 1 (Title / Hook)

A cinematic, wide 16:9 hero shot: a person's hand holding a smartphone in a
bustling Manila street market at golden hour, the phone screen glowing with a
clean fintech payment-confirmation UI. In the near background, a small merchant
stall displays a printed QR code on its counter. Warm cyan-to-orange gradient
light bathes the scene, evoking a modern, optimistic payments brand. Shallow
depth of field, phone and QR code in sharp focus, crowd softly blurred behind.
Photorealistic, premium fintech advertising style, high dynamic range, no visible
text or logos on the phone. Leave the lower-left third relatively clean and
uncluttered so a title overlay ("HeyPay") reads clearly against it.

## Image Prompt #2 — Slide 2 (Problem)

A conceptual split-world illustration, wide 16:9, expressing two payment worlds
that cannot connect. Left half: a warm, human Philippine street-merchant scene
with a peso/QRPH bank-payment motif (a shop QR code, a bank building icon),
rendered in warm orange tones. Right half: a cool, abstract crypto world — a
glowing Stellar/XLM coin and blockchain node lattice in cyan-blue tones. Between
them, a stark visible gap or chasm with a broken or missing bridge, emphasizing
disconnection. Clean modern flat-vector editorial illustration style, strong
central negative space, minimal detail, muted background so a short headline can
sit at the top. Mood: tension of a missing link, waiting to be solved.
